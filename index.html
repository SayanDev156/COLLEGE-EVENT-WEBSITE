About The Event

CampusFest is a student-first ecosystem built to celebrate innovation, leadership, creativity, and collaboration. This year features AI & robotics showcases, coding arenas, cultural battles, design sprints, and startup pitch sessions.

Whether you are a developer, designer, entrepreneur, performer, or enthusiast, there is a stage for you.
